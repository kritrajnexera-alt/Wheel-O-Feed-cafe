# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Ankleshwar locals and visitors (18–40) seeking a premium cafe experience. Young professionals on breaks, college students hanging out, families dining out, and coffee enthusiasts. They value quality food, great ambience, and Instagram-worthy presentation. Primary context: leisure, socializing, and casual dining.

## Product Purpose

Showcase Wheel O Feed as Ankleshwar's #1 premium multi-cuisine cafe. Drive foot traffic, Instagram engagement, and WhatsApp orders. Communicate the cafe's premium positioning (₹860–₹2,100/person), cozy industrial vibe, diverse menu, and barista-grade coffee. Success = visitors feel compelled to visit, follow on Instagram, or order via WhatsApp.

## Brand Personality

Warm, premium, confident. Industrial coffee-shop edge with a welcoming local heart. Professional but never cold. Three words: **earthy · bold · cozy**

## Anti-references

- No purple gradients, pastels, or SaaS-like corporate pastels
- No generic 3-column icon grids with rounded pastel cards
- No Inter-only font stacks (must pair Space Grotesk + Inter)
- No glassmorphism as default decoration
- No AI-template tells: no numbered section eyebrows (01 About, 02 Process), no gradient text, no side-stripe borders
- No cream/sand/beige body backgrounds — using deep espresso brown instead

## Design Principles

1. **Warmth through depth, not tint** — the dark espresso background carries emotion; amber accents provide heat and energy
2. **Every pixel intentional** — no decorative flourishes that don't serve the brand story or user action
3. **Typography as texture** — bold Space Grotesk headings + clean Inter body create the premium editorial feel
4. **Motion with restraint** — animations enhance storytelling (hero entrance, scroll reveals) but never distract
5. **Local pride, global taste** — rooted in Ankleshwar GIDC identity, serving world cuisines

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (body text ≥4.5:1 contrast against espresso background)
- Amber accent on dark bg passes large-text threshold (>3:1)
- Reduced-motion alternatives via prefers-reduced-motion media queries
- Clear focus states on all interactive elements
- Readable font sizes (never below 14px for body)
